#### I'm a Product Designer who codes

Simply put, I’m a software engineer turned product designer, building at the intersection of design, code, and human-computer interaction (HCI). I don’t just mock up interfaces in Figma ,I engineer polished, visually engaging experiences across both web and native platforms.

After a brief sabbatical from building in public, I’m back. This GitHub is where I share personal open-source projects, UI components, experiments, and small useful apps many inspired by ideas I post on X and Dribbble.

I’ve always been driven to build useful products that solve relatable, real-world problems. Right now, I’m especially interested in social applications and systems that reduce friction in manufacturing, commerce, and finance.

Turning “ugh” into “wow” with design and technology? I’m obsessed.
